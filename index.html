McDonald's Menu

McDonald's offers a variety of classic fast food items that have become globally recognized. Some popular items include:

  • Big Mac: A double-decker burger with two beef patties, lettuce, cheese, pickles, onions, and a special sauce.
  • McChicken: A crispy chicken sandwich with lettuce and mayonnaise on a soft bun.
  • French Fries: Golden, crispy fries that are famous worldwide.
  • Egg McMuffin: A popular breakfast sandwich made with egg, Canadian bacon, and cheese on an English muffin.

McDonald's focuses on providing quick meals that range from burgers and fries to breakfast items and desserts like the McFlurry.
